Black Forest Cake Recipe


Photo by Jacob Thomas on Unsplash

Here's a classic Black Forest Cake (Schwarzwälder Kirschtorte) recipe with layers of chocolate sponge cake, whipped cream, cherries, and a hint of kirsch (cherry brandy).

Ingredients:

For the Chocolate Sponge Cake:

  • 1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup (50g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 ½ cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • ½ cup (120ml) vegetable oil (or melted butter)
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup (120ml) buttermilk (or milk + ½ tbsp vinegar)

For the Cherry Filling:

  • 2 cups (about 500g) pitted cherries
  • ¼ cup (50g) sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp kirsch (cherry brandy, optional)

For the Whipped Cream Frosting:

  • 3 cups (720ml) heavy cream, chilled
  • ½ cup (60g) powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp kirsch (optional)

For Decoration:

  • Chocolate shavings or curls
  • Fresh cherries
  • Extra whipped cream

Steps:

  1. Bake the Chocolate Cake:

    • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line two 8-inch (20cm) round cake pans.
    • In a bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
    • In another bowl, beat eggs and sugar until light and fluffy (~5 mins). Add oil, vanilla, and buttermilk, mixing well.
    • Gradually fold in the dry ingredients until just combined.
    • Divide batter between pans and bake for 25-30 mins (until a toothpick comes out clean). Cool completely.

  2. Prepare the Cherry Filling:

    • In a saucepan, cook cherries, sugar, and cornstarch over medium heat until thickened (~5-7 mins). Stir in kirsch (if using). Cool completely.

  3. Make the Whipped Cream:

    • Whip heavy cream, powdered sugar, vanilla, and kirsch until stiff peaks form.

  4. Assemble the Cake:

    • Slice each cake layer in half horizontally to make 4 layers.
    • Place the first layer on a plate, brush with kirsch (optional), then spread whipped cream and add cherry filling.
    • Repeat with remaining layers.
    • Frost the outside with whipped cream and press chocolate shavings onto the sides.
    • Decorate the top with cream swirls, cherries, and more chocolate.

  5. Chill & Serve:

    • Refrigerate for at least 4 hours (preferably overnight) before slicing.
